Natalia's Shaved Ice

Natalia’s Shaved Ice has been a local favorite in Homestead, Florida for the past eight years. They are at the Redland’s Farmer’s Market every weekend and do big events from Florida City to Miami as well as a lot of fundraising for schools and churches. They are known for their wide variety of products made with homemade flavors and fresh fruit. When re-branding their business Natalia wanted something to reflect their old school style. We decided to go with a vintage hand lettering feel that was simple but still showed the nature of the business.
